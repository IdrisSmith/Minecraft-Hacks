Mako The Pig

Mako is a very special pig, who lives in southwestern Buffalo. His owner Blanqueador, migrated from eastern Equador for greater opprotunity. But without a knowledge of English and American customs, Blanqueador had no chance but to go into farming. At first, he only raised cows and chickens, as he could make the most money off of them. But one day, a small piglet wandered onto his farm. Blanqueador raised the pig, planning to eat him when the time was right. Blanqueador made one mistake, he named the piglet. Once Mako had matured, Blanqueador could bring himself to kill Mako. Mako, realizing Blanqueador's original intentions, used his hypnotic powers to mind control Blanqueador. Gaze upon his powers with caution.
